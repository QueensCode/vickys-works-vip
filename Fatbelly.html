HOW TO MAKE BREAD

Simple way to make bread, Feb 7, 2021
Soft Bread

There is a very easy way to make your bread at home. My desire is to teach you how to make and bake your bread using an oven or stove. Enjoy!

INGREDIENTS
  • 2 cups Wheat Flour
  • 100grams Butter
  • 100grams Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Yeast
  • 5 grams Milk
  • 2 Eggs

Steps To Follow:

  • In a bowl, dissolve the 1 tbsp of sugar in warm water, add the yeast and mix, allow to proof until yeast becomes creamy and foamy. It should take 5mins at most.

  • Add the remaining sugar, melted butter, salt and milk into the mixture and add your sieved flour to form a dough

  • Knead the dough for 7 minutes.

  • Cover the dough and allow to rise for 30 minutes in a warm dry place.

  • Knead again for 5 minutes.

  • Cover and allow for 20 minutes

  • Punch dough down.

  • Using a bread baking pan of your choice, cut the dough into the desired size(s) put in the pan.

  • Glaze the dough with milk wash or egg.

  • Bake at 350 degrees f (175 degrees c)for 30-40 minutes in an oven. For those using a stove, bake with a pan on the stove for 30 minutes, flip the bread and bake for another 20 minutes.

HOW TO COOK OHA SOUP

Oha Soup, December 20, 2021
Oha Soup

Oha soup is a favourite amongst the Igbo people in Nigeria.

Ingredients.
  • One bunch of oha leaves
  • Uziza leaves-Optional
  • Crayfish
  • Dried Fish
  • Stockfish
  • Beef or Chicken
  • Stock cubes
  • Ogiri/fermented Locust beans
  • Palm Oil
  • Dried Perper
  • Cocoyam
  • Onions
  • Palm Oil
Cooking Method:
  • Wash the cocoyam and bring to a boil

  • Peel the cooked cocoyam and pound into a paste

  • Wash the beef or chicken, season and steam until well cooked

  • Add the well washed Stockfish

  • Add the cocoyam paste, stir for 3 minutes until completely dissolved

  • Add the Palm Oil and stir

  • Pluck the oha leaves, shred with your hands into tiny bits, wash and set aside.

  • Add pepper, crayfish, onions, ogiri, stock cubes and boil for about 10 minutes.

  • Stir and add the dried fish, boil for 5 minutes, stir and add the oha leaves.

  • Set aside and Serve with any swallow of your choice
